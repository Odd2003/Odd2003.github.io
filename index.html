A bit about me

I'm an iOS developer who never quite gave up drawing. Most of what I ship has art I made myself in it — an icon set, a mascot, a loading state that's a tiny animation instead of a spinner.

I like small, finished things more than big, unfinished ones, which is why you'll find shipped apps next to rough prototypes here — both matter to how I work.
